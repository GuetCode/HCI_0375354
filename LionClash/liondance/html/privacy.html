The information collected is strictly used for event-related purposes such as participant registration, communication, scheduling updates, and competition coordination. We may also use your details to send important announcements, reminders, and results regarding the contest.

We are committed to ensuring your data is securely stored and protected. All personal information is handled with strict confidentiality and protected using appropriate security measures. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you may contact us at info@liondance.com. We will respond to all inquiries as soon as possible.
