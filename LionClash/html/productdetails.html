Contest Details

Welcome to the ultimate arena of rhythm, agility, and power. Our championships bring together top-tier troupes from around the world to compete across four distinct, high-octane divisions. Discover the unique styles that define modern competitive Lion Dance.

Categories Overview

➽Junior Category:

A foundation stage for young rising performers (Under 16).

The Junior Category is designed to nurture young talent in lion dance performance. It focuses on basic techniques, discipline, teamwork, rhythm control, and confidence building. Judges evaluate potential, coordination, and growth rather than extreme difficulty.

➽Open Category:

Elite competition for advanced and professional teams.

The Open Category is the highest competitive level where teams showcase advanced stunts, powerful choreography, and elite-level synchronization. Creativity, difficulty, and audience impact are heavily weighted in scoring.

➽Traditional Category:

Preserving cultural heritage and authentic lion dance style.

This category emphasizes cultural authenticity, traditional movements, historical storytelling, and respect for heritage. Teams must follow classical lion dance structure with minimal modern interpretation.

Judging Criteria

Performance Skills

Accuracy of movements, coordination, rhythm, and stability during routine execution.

Creativity

Original choreography, innovation in storytelling, and uniqueness of presentation.

Technical Difficulty

Complexity of stunts, balance control, and execution under pressure.

Team Synchronization

Unity between performers, timing precision, and group coordination.

Stage Presence

Confidence, audience engagement, and overall performance energy.
