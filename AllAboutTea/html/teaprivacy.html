Privacy Policy

How Jade Dynasty handles guest information for tea bookings, enquiries, publications, and cultural event communication.

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Introduction

Jade Dynasty respects your privacy and keeps personal information limited to what is needed for tea-house enquiries, event reservations, product communication, and guest support.

Information Collection

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, event details, enquiry message, and preferences shared when contacting the tea house.

Information Usage

Information is used to respond to enquiries, manage bookings, share requested tea-house updates, and improve guest service.

Information Protection

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect guest information from unauthorised access or misuse.

Cookies

This static template may use basic cookies or analytics only if enabled by the site owner. Cookie behaviour should be adjusted to match the final CMS setup.

Third Party Links

External links such as social media pages are controlled by their own privacy policies and are not managed by Jade Dynasty.

User Rights

Guests may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information by contacting the tea house directly.
