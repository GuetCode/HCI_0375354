Our Tea Services

Traditional tea experiences crafted for ceremonies, tastings, workshops, gifting, and cultural learning.

Traditional Tea Ceremony

A calm guided ritual centred on fragrant leaves, warm vessels, and attentive serving.

Guests are introduced to brewing rhythm, cup warming, aroma appreciation, and respectful serving practices in a quiet red-and-gold tea-house setting.

Tea Tasting Flight

Compare oolong, green tea, black tea, white tea, and aged pu-erh in one tasting.

A tea master explains origin, processing style, aroma, mouthfeel, steeping rhythm, and suggested food pairings for each tea.

Private Cultural Workshop

Hands-on learning for brewing, teaware care, etiquette, and tea appreciation.

Participants learn how water, temperature, vessel shape, leaf ratio, and steeping time change the character of the final cup.
